About LAM

What you Need to Know

We are based in Pembury, Kent but work throughout the UK. Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) was founded in 2009 by Director Mark Richards. Mark has over 30 years’ experience in waterproofing and surfacing industries and has managed many prestigious commercial and domestic projects throughout the UK. Mark’s tenacious attitude can add value to any project along with his wealth of project management expertise whilst his eye for detail and efficiency ensure all services are carried out on time and on budget.

Liquid Applied Membranes deliver proven solutions to fulfil a wide range of applications. Our systems deliver the highest levels of technical performance. We offer all of our customers both commercial and domestic the highest levels of support and service in satisfying their specific needs. We pride ourselves on our customer service which results in high customer satisfaction and repeat business

Liquid Applied Membranes operates within the commercial, industrial and domestic sectors. We work with our clients in the most efficient and cost effective way throughout their projects. LAM can add value to any project by tailoring value engineering solutions to help the client save on cost whilst maximising performance. LAM is responsible for sales, estimating, design and installation.

Our Team

All of our operatives hold CSCS cards and receive extensive health and safety training. Our waterproofing and protection systems are only installed by our own fully trained and authorised contractors. We invest in directly employed teams of site operatives, site managers and project managers. We ensure all of our staff are trained to the highest possible standard by arranging regular on-site training with our long term partner material manufacturers. We believe in keeping our staff happy to ensure we maximise satisfaction for our customers. There is always an operations manager and site manager on hand to assist. All of the team pride themselves on reliability.

LAM believe in complete client satisfaction and are committed to ensure we always maintain and develop our business relationships whilst maintaining high standards and customer service, regardless of the size of the project. If you would like any assistance with your projects we would welcome hearing from you and feel sure you will find our rates highly competitive. Our dedicated team can help you with all areas of your projects including:

  • Site surveys
  • Quotations
  • Samples
  • Technical advice
  • On and off site testing
