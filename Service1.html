Tank Lining Service

The Facts

Water storage tank inspection is a vital part of the planned maintenance to prevent critical storage tanks from failing, potentially causing injury or costing the company millions to repair or replace.

Wet areas around any water storage tanks are amongst one of the signs you will see if there is an issue with your tank lining, it is important to call LAM as soon as possible, as you can avoid replacing your tank

A leaking water storage tank or cooling tower coating repair is far more cost-effective than water tank replacement and downtime of water services and general disruption to your business is minimal.

The Solution for containment and industrial tank leaks

LAM GRP Lining and Coatings System is suitable to protect your business assets from the major problems associated with Industrial and Commercial water storage leaks.

  • Water Tank Lining failure
  • Water Tank Leak
  • Sectional Tank Lining
  • Underground Tank Lining leaking
  • GRP Tank Leak

With over 20 years experience in fibreglass lining, our proprietary GRP Products are supplied on site to your exact specifications to deliver optimum performance and a substantial saving on tank replacement.
